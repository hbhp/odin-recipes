Pancakes

Description

Ingredients

  • 100g Plain flour
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 300ml Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Sunflower or vegetable oil

Optional

  • Lemon wedeges (To serve)
  • Caster sugar (To serve)

Steps

  1. Bring a small saucepan of water to a rapid boil. Whisk the water to create a vortex then crack the egg into the centre and cook for 3 minutes. Remove the egg with a slotted spoon and place into a bowl of iced water.
  2. Meanwhile, toast the bread.
  3. Plate up and serve.
