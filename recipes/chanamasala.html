Chana Masala Recipe

Description

Serve this vegetarian Indian chickpea curry as a main meal or side dish. It's gluten-free, good for you and freezable so you can save any leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 400gdried chickpeas (or 4 x 400g tinned chickpeas, drained)
  • Salt
  • 3-4 Tbsp Rapeseed Oil
  • 3 Large Onions
  • 6cm Ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 4 Cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 2 Green chillis, chopped
  • 2 Tins of Plum Tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 2 Tsp Garam masala
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Turmeric
